About Me

I am a Ph.D. graduate in the Vision and Learning Lab, under the guidance of Prof. Ming-Hsuan Yang. My research focuses on deep learning and machine learning for computer vision and computer graphics, with an emphasis on multi-view video generation using generative AI and 3D/4D modeling techniques.
